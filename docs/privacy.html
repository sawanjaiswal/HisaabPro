Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

HisaabPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the HisaabPro mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information (name, phone number, email address)
  • Business information (business name, address, GSTIN)
  • Transaction data (invoices, payments, inventory records)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers)
  • Log data (access times, pages viewed, app crashes)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's core billing, inventory, and payment features
  • To create and manage your account
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send transactional notifications (payment reminders, invoice updates)
  • To improve our app and develop new features
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Your business data is stored locally on your device (offline-first) and synced to our secure servers when connected. We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit and AES-256 for data at rest. We do not sell your personal or business data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing (governed by Razorpay's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Analytics / Firebase — for app analytics and crash reporting

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or law enforcement requests
  • To protect the rights and safety of HisaabPro and its users
  • With service providers who assist in operating our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be removed from our servers within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Children's Privacy

HisaabPro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
