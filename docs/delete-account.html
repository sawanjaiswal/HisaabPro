Delete Your HisaabPro Account

Last updated: April 10, 2026

You have the right to request deletion of your HisaabPro account and all associated personal data at any time. This page explains how the deletion process works and what data is removed.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1 — In-app request (recommended):

  1. Open the HisaabPro app and sign in
  2. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account
  3. Confirm your decision by entering your password
  4. Tap Delete My Account

Option 2 — Email request:

Send an email to support@hisaabpro.in from the email address associated with your HisaabPro account. Please include:

  • Your registered name
  • Your registered phone number
  • Your business name
  • The subject line: Account Deletion Request
Request Deletion by Email

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove the following from our systems within 30 days:

  • Your account profile (name, email, phone, password hash)
  • Your business profile (business name, address, GSTIN, logo)
  • All invoices, payments, customers, and inventory records created under your account
  • All generated PDFs, receipts, and exported documents stored on our servers
  • Your authentication sessions, refresh tokens, and device identifiers
  • Any support correspondence linked to your account

What We Retain (and Why)

For legal, tax, and fraud-prevention reasons, we retain the following for a limited period after deletion:

  • Anonymized transaction logs — kept for up to 7 years as required by Indian tax and accounting regulations. These contain no personally identifiable information.
  • Payment records — if you used paid features, Razorpay transaction IDs are retained for up to 7 years per RBI guidelines, with personal data redacted.
  • Abuse/fraud records — if your account was suspended for policy violations, a minimal record is retained to prevent re-registration.
  • Server backups — deletion propagates to encrypted backups within 90 days as backup rotations complete.

Partial Deletion

HisaabPro does not currently support partial deletion — when you delete your account, all associated data is removed together. If you want to delete only specific records (a single invoice, a specific customer), you can do so individually from within the app before deleting your account.

Timeline

  • Immediately: Your account is deactivated and you are signed out of all devices.
  • Within 24 hours: Your data is soft-deleted and no longer accessible from the app.
  • Within 30 days: Your data is hard-deleted from active production databases.
  • Within 90 days: Your data is removed from all encrypted backup copies.

Cancelling a Deletion Request

If you change your mind, you have 7 days after submitting a deletion request to cancel it. Contact support@hisaabpro.in from your registered email within 7 days and we will restore your account.

After 7 days, deletion is irreversible and your data cannot be recovered.

Before you delete: Consider exporting your data first. Go to Settings → Data → Export to download your invoices, customers, and inventory as CSV or PDF files. Exported files remain yours and are not affected by account deletion.

Questions?

If you have any questions about account deletion or data handling, please contact us at support@hisaabpro.in.
